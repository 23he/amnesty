Configuring a Widget

Control-click a widget and select Configure from the popup menu that appears.

Window

    Opacity. The opacity of the widget: from almost invisible to fully opaque.

    Level. The level the widget is placed into: Floating (above all other windows), Standard (in the same level as other windows) and Desktop (below all other windows, embedded inside the desktop).

    Lock in place. Locks the widget into its current location in the layout.

    Ignore clicks. Puts the widget in a "click-though" mode where mouse clicks pass right through it. Select Bring Layout to Front in the Layout submenu to override this setting for all widgets in the current Layout.

Advanced

    Auto update. If the widget is designed to update when shown, this setting forces the widget to update (refresh its information) at specific time intervals.

    Size. The size of the widget: from 50% to 150%, in increments of 25%.

    Rotation. The rotation of the widget: from 0 to 360 degrees around the center of the widget.

Note: widgets that are scaled or rotated may not consistenly respond to the mouse when clicked, especially when dragged. Depending on your settings, you may have to discover an "anchor" point that you can use to move a widget with your particular rotation and/or size settings.
